# Code of conduct

We expect people in the Envman community to be respectful, specific, and constructive. Do not harass, discriminate against, intimidate, or publish another person's private information.

## Working together

Assume good faith, ask for evidence when a claim is unclear, and keep technical disagreement focused on the change rather than the person. Maintainers may moderate discussions, issues, and pull requests to keep them useful and safe.

## Reporting concerns

Do not put personal information or conduct concerns in a public issue. Submit a private [Crux Experts support ticket](https://my.cruxexperts.com/submitticket.php?step=2&deptid=1) with the subject `Envman conduct report` and include only the information needed to understand what happened. Use [private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/CruxExperts/envman/security/advisories/new) for security vulnerabilities; use [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) for non-sensitive routing.
